About Michael Toth

Find me online:

Michael Toth is currently working in investment data analytics and living in New York City. Today, Michael is working at Orchard where he is the Manager of Investment and Credit Strategy. In his role at Orchard, Michael analyzes the loan details of many loan originators and helps investors to build investment strategies in the marketplace lending space. Prior to his role with Orchard, Michael was working at BlackRock where he specialized in securitized product valuation and risk analytics.

Michael is passionate about using data and statistics to solve challenging business problems and answer interesting questions. Specicically, Michael is interested in machine learning, data analysis, data visualization, and applied statistics. Michael is experienced in R, Python, and Javascript.

Michael graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 2011, where he received a BSc in Economics with concentrations in statistics and finance and a minor in mathematics.

When he's not programming or working, Michael enjoys working out, playing chess, and watching movies. If you'd like to get in contact, please reach out via Twitter or LinkedIn.
